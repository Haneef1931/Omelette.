This is a simple omelette recipe and i am going to show you how to make it

An easy and quick dish pefect for any meal.This classic omelette combineds beaten eggs cookes to perfection, optional fillings to your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

    Peperation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preperation: 5 minutes
  • . Cooking: 5 minutes

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Peper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of Butter or Oil
  • fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meat,herbs.

Instructions

1. beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.

2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over meduim heat and add Butter or Oil

3. Cook the Omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbiling, por the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

4. Add fillings(Optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choice of fillings over half of the Omelette.

5. Fold and Serve: As the Omelette continue to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Lats cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and peppper if needed.
